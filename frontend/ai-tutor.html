Minallo AI Tutor

An AI study assistant that actually reads your course material. Import lecture PDFs, exercise sheets, and formula collections, and Minallo's AI will answer questions, solve problems step-by-step, and explain concepts using your documents — not a generic textbook.

What makes it course-aware

  • Import from Course attaches real PDF text to the chat — the AI sees it on every reply.
  • Step-by-step problem solving with formulas in LaTeX and reasoning you can follow.
  • Cited answers — the AI points back to the lecture or exercise it pulled from.
  • Summaries & flashcards generated from any selection or uploaded document.
  • Honest when context is missing — it tells you what it doesn't know instead of making things up.

How students use it

Solving exercise sheets without staring at a single line for 40 minutes. Catching up on a missed lecture by uploading the slides and asking for the key points. Building a flashcard deck the night before an exam from one command. Asking "explain this formula again" while reading a PDF.
