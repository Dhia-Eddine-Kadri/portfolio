Frequently Asked Questions

Questions Students Ask Before Trying Minallo

Clear answers about course files, study guidance, uploaded materials, pricing, and exam preparation.

Is Minallo just another AI chatbot?

No. Minallo is built around your course and your study workflow. You can upload your course materials, ask questions about them, generate summaries, create flashcards, test yourself with quizzes, build cheatsheets, practice with ExamForge, and learn difficult topics step by step with Deep Learn.

Minallo is not just chat. It is a complete exam-prep workspace.

Does Minallo answer from my uploaded course files?

Yes. Minallo focuses first on your uploaded course materials, because those are usually the files your exam is based on.

This helps you study from your own lectures, notes, exercises, and PDFs instead of getting generic answers that may not match your course.

Does Minallo cite where the answer comes from?

Minallo is designed to give course-grounded answers and show where the information comes from when possible. This helps you check the answer against your own lecture material and study with more confidence.

What happens if Minallo does not have enough context?

If the needed information is missing, Minallo should be honest about it. Instead of inventing an answer, it can tell you what is missing and ask for the right file, page, lecture, or exercise number.

This is important because good studying is not only about getting answers quickly. It is also about knowing when more context is needed.

Can Minallo help me know what to study next?

Yes. Minallo can help you move from a pile of PDFs to a clearer study path. It can recommend when to start with a Cheatsheet, when to revise with Flashcards, when to test yourself with Quiz, when to practice with ExamForge, and when to use Deep Learn for difficult topics.

The goal is to help you stop guessing and start studying with a plan.

Can Minallo prepare me for an exam?

Minallo helps you prepare actively by turning your own course materials into study tools. You can upload your files, generate a Cheatsheet, revise with Flashcards, test yourself with Quiz, practice with ExamForge, and learn difficult topics with Deep Learn.

Minallo does not guarantee exam results, but it helps you study in a more structured and active way.

Can Minallo identify weak topics?

Minallo can help you notice weak areas through your quizzes, flashcards, practice, and study activity. It can then suggest what to review next and which study tool to use.

For example, you can review a weak topic with Deep Learn, create flashcards for it, or practice similar questions with ExamForge.

Can Minallo handle scanned slides, handwritten notes, or diagrams?

Minallo works best with clear, readable files. Text-based PDFs usually give the best results. Scanned slides, handwritten notes, and diagram-heavy documents depend on how clear and readable the upload is.

For best results, upload sharp, high-quality files where the text is not blurry and the pages are correctly rotated.

What should I upload to Minallo?

You can upload lecture notes, PDFs, slides, exercises, summaries, formula sheets, scripts, and other course materials.

The clearer and more organized your files are, the better Minallo can help you study from them.

Can I upload multiple files for one course?

Yes. Minallo is built around course workspaces, so you can keep multiple files inside one course and use them to generate summaries, quizzes, flashcards, cheatsheets, Deep Learn explanations, and ExamForge practice.

What is Cheatsheet?

Cheatsheet turns long course materials into compact revision notes. It helps you quickly review the most important definitions, formulas, concepts, and key ideas before an exam.

What is ExamForge?

ExamForge helps you practice with exam-style questions based on your course materials. It is designed for students who want to move beyond reading and prepare more actively for the exam.

What is Deep Learn?

Deep Learn is Minallo's guided learning mode. It helps you understand difficult topics step by step instead of only giving you a short answer.

It can explain the idea, break it down, give examples, and help you check whether you understood it.

What are Flashcards for?

Flashcards help you revise with active recall. Minallo can turn important definitions, formulas, concepts, and questions from your course into cards you can use for faster revision.

What is Quiz for?

Quiz helps you test your understanding before the exam. Instead of only rereading your notes, you can answer questions, find gaps, and review the topics you still struggle with.

Can Minallo replace studying?

No. Minallo does not replace studying. It helps you study smarter by organizing your material, explaining difficult topics, creating revision tools, and helping you practice actively.

You still need to review, understand, and practice the material yourself.

Are AI answers always correct?

AI can make mistakes, so important answers should always be checked with your course material, lecture notes, exercise sheets, or professor's instructions.

Minallo is designed to help you study faster and more clearly, but you should still use your own judgment for exam preparation.

Can Minallo help if I only have a few days before the exam?

Yes. Minallo can help you focus quickly by turning your material into Cheatsheets, Flashcards, Quiz questions, and ExamForge practice. It can also help you review difficult topics with Deep Learn.

This is useful when you need a clear study plan instead of wasting time deciding where to start.

You can try Pro free for 7 days. After the trial, it costs €11.99/month. No hidden feature upsells.

Yes. The Pro plan is monthly and can be cancelled anytime before the next billing period. You can also cancel during the 7-day free trial if you do not want to continue.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Minallo offers a 7-day free trial, so you can try the Pro study tools before the monthly subscription starts.

Is my course material private?

Your uploaded course materials are your study files. Minallo should treat them as private and use them only to help you study inside your own account.

Who is Minallo for?

Minallo is for students who want to study smarter from their own course materials. It is especially useful during exam season, when you need to organize your files, understand difficult topics, revise efficiently, and practice actively.
