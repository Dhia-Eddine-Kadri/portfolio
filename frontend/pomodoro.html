Why a Pomodoro timer matters for studying

The Pomodoro technique works because it pairs short bursts of focus with mandatory rest — which is how attention actually behaves. Minallo's timer sits next to the work, so the cycle includes "read the lecture, ask the AI a question, take the break" instead of forcing you to switch apps.
